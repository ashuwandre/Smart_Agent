# Network Outage and Service Credit Policy

**Policy version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-01

## Outage handling

Check the outage system using the customer's region before declaring an outage. If an active incident exists, provide only the incident status and estimated restoration time returned by that system. If no incident is recorded, do not claim the network is healthy; complete router checks and create a technical ticket when appropriate.

For a suspected unrecorded outage affecting multiple customers, escalate to network operations. Never invent a cause or restoration time.

## Service credits

A verified continuous provider-side outage must exceed 24 hours before a credit is eligible. The credit equals the affected full days of the customer's monthly fee, calculated using a 30-day month, and is capped at 50% of one monthly fee. Planned maintenance announced at least 48 hours in advance is not automatically creditable.

Credits up to ₹1,000 may be applied after outage duration and eligibility are verified. A larger credit requires human approval under the Refund Policy threshold. Tell the customer when a credit is pending verification rather than promising it.
