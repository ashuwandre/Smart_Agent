# Billing and Invoice Policy

**Policy version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-01

## Billing cycle

Subscriptions are billed in advance on the customer's renewal date. The monthly fee is determined by the active plan at the time the invoice is generated. Taxes or clearly identified usage charges may appear as separate invoice lines.

## Invoice questions

Before explaining a charge, verify the customer record and the relevant invoice. State the plan, billing period, base fee, and any additional line items shown by the billing data. Never invent a fee or adjustment that is not present in the account or invoice record.

## Duplicate and failed payments

A charge is considered a possible duplicate only when two successful transactions have the same customer, amount, and billing period. A temporary bank authorization is not a settled duplicate charge. Verified duplicates follow the Refund Policy.

Failed payments may be retried for up to 7 days. An account with an unpaid invoice may be marked `past_due`; service can be suspended after the retry period. A payment can take up to 24 hours to appear in the account. If it remains missing after 24 hours, create a billing ticket with the payment reference—do not ask for full card or bank credentials.

## Plan changes

Upgrades take effect immediately unless the customer explicitly selects the next billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal. Any price or entitlement claim must match the Plans and Entitlements document.
