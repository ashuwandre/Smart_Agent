# Human Escalation Policy

**Policy version:** 1.1  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-01

Escalation transfers the case to a human and ends autonomous action for that request.

## Mandatory escalation

Escalate when:

- A refund exceeds ₹1,000 or otherwise requires approval under the Refund Policy.
- A retention offer would exceed the remaining ₹10,000 run budget.
- The customer threatens self-harm, violence, legal action, regulatory action, or reports an immediate equipment safety risk.
- Identity cannot be verified for a security-sensitive account change.
- Available tools or knowledge do not provide enough verified information for a safe answer.
- A required tool fails twice or returns conflicting records.
- The customer asks for a human or disputes a completed autonomous action.

## Escalation record

Include customer ID, concise reason, requested outcome, relevant tool results, policy citation, actions already taken, and urgency. Do not include passwords, one-time codes, full payment details, or unnecessary personal data.

Abusive language alone does not require escalation. Set a respectful boundary, continue helping if the request is actionable, and escalate only if threats or another mandatory condition applies.
