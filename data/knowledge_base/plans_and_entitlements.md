# Plans and Entitlements

**Catalog version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-01

## Current monthly plans

- **Basic — ₹299/month:** SD streaming on 1 device and email support.
- **Standard — ₹599/month:** HD streaming on 2 devices, cloud DVR, email support, and chat support.
- **Premium — ₹999/month:** UHD streaming on 4 devices, cloud DVR, offline downloads, and priority support.
- **Business — ₹1,999/month:** UHD streaming on up to 10 devices, admin console, usage reports, and dedicated support.

Taxes may apply and appear separately on the invoice. Do not promise an entitlement that is not listed for the selected plan.

## Changes

An upgrade takes effect immediately by default; the customer may instead request activation at the next billing cycle. A downgrade takes effect at the next renewal. Before changing a plan, state the new monthly fee, the effective date, and the important entitlement changes, then obtain explicit customer confirmation.

For a Basic customer who needs HD on two devices, Standard is the minimum suitable plan. For offline downloads or UHD, Premium or Business is required.
