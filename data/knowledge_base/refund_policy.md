# Refund Policy

**Policy version:** 1.0  
**Effective date:** 2026-07-01

## Eligible refunds

A payment may be refunded when one of the following is verified:

- The same subscription charge was processed more than once.
- A customer cancelled before renewal but was charged after the recorded cancellation time.
- A renewal occurred within the last 7 calendar days and no paid service was used after renewal.
- Support confirms a continuous service outage longer than 24 hours; service credits are handled under the Network Outage Policy.

A change of mind after 7 days, partial use of a billing period, or dissatisfaction without a verified service failure is not automatically refundable.

## Approval and amount limits

- Refunds up to and including ₹1,000 may be issued autonomously only after eligibility and the original charge are verified.
- Any single refund above ₹1,000 requires human approval before execution.
- Multiple refunds must not be split to avoid the approval threshold.
- The refund must never exceed the verified charge being reversed.

## Processing

Approved refunds return to the original payment method. Processing normally takes 5–7 business days. The agent must state that bank processing times can vary and must provide the related invoice or transaction reference when available.
